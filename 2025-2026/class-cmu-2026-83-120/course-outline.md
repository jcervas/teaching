## UNIT I — History

### Central Questions
- How is the federal judiciary organized, and what kinds of power does it exercise?
- What political, legal, and philosophical ideas shaped the U.S. Constitution?
- What purposes do constitutions serve, and how do they structure and constrain political power?
- How did colonial experience and revolutionary politics influence constitutional design?
- How has constitutional meaning evolved across major historical periods?
- What role has the Supreme Court played in shaping constitutional development?
- To what extent is constitutional law shaped by politics, history, and institutional context?
